Solomon Rodd

My agenda as Graduate Student Council Representative:

  • More opportunities to network and connect with students, beyond quorum call, more structured and funded
  • Improve management of course enrollment and simplify process
  • More small class and seminar options
  • More Harvard-wide opportunities for graduate students to gather and connect with other Harvard schools
  • Better integration activities between MPA, MPP, and MC-MPA, including days of joint orientation
  • More events with programs and professors from other Harvard schools
  • Opportunities for furnished Harvard housing apartments for HKS students
  • Advisors or program staff knowledgeable about resources at other schools at Harvard that interest HKS students
  • Better coffee in the lounge, longer cafeteria hours, better food at activities, free donuts
  • Consolidating all opportunities for funding/traveling, clubs, centers, and initiatives in one place by deadline
  • Increase options for international-related classes and studies
  • More campus sustainability initiatives to reduce waste and make Harvard greener
  • Facilitate unity, support between students, and spaces for non-popular thoughts to be heard
  • Improve controls of local room temperature to maintain comfort levels for all
  • More information during orientation periods on course selection and opportunities at HKS, including Centers
  • Improve availability of books in the library for full access to electronic versions
  • Improve career services with more tailored support, expanding mentorship, internships, and alumni connections
  • Campus Facilities and Technology: More phone booths, study spaces, and collaboration tools
